Welcome To The Portfolio Of Richard McFarlane

Some of my favorite video games over the years have been games such as Halo 3, Call of Duty MW2, Pokemon Soul Silver and currently Warframe. I've been playing video games since I was a little kid so they have become a large part of my life.

When I was a kid my dad used to take me for rides on his motorcycle so when I turned 16 I decided to get my lisence so i could ride them myself. My first motorcycle was a 1988 katana 600 and i currently drive a 1986 virago 750.
